# Legal OS Design Brainstorm

## Design Approach Selected: Modern Professional with Legal Authority

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Legal Tech + Minimalist Precision

**Core Principles:**
1. **Authoritative Clarity** — Clean typography and structured layouts that communicate legal precision and trustworthiness
2. **Intelligent Hierarchy** — Visual weight guides users through complex workflows without cognitive overload
3. **Purposeful Restraint** — Minimal ornamentation; every element serves a function in the legal workflow
4. **Accessible Professionalism** — High contrast, readable typography, and intuitive navigation for time-pressured lawyers

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Primary: Deep Navy (#1a2847)** — Trust, authority, legal tradition
- **Accent: Tealime (#56CCF2)** — Intelligence, AI assistance, modern tech
- **Success: Charge Green (#A8E063)** — Positive outcomes, approved actions
- **Warning: Amber (#FF9800)** — Risk alerts, contract issues
- **Background: Off-white (#F8F9FA)** — Reduces eye strain during long work sessions
- **Dark Mode: Charcoal (#1F2937)** — Professional dark theme for evening work

**Layout Paradigm:**
- **Asymmetric Dashboard:** Left sidebar for navigation, main content area with card-based modules, right panel for contextual information
- **Command-Driven Interface:** Top command bar for quick actions (/analyze contract, /explore due diligence)
- **Workflow Visualization:** Progress indicators and timeline views for multi-step legal processes
- **Responsive Grid:** 12-column grid with flexible card sizing for different screen sizes

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Risk Indicator Badges** — Color-coded risk levels (High/Medium/Low) with exposure amounts
2. **Agent Activity Timeline** — Vertical timeline showing AI agent actions with timestamps
3. **Document Comparison View** — Side-by-side viewer for original vs. redlined contracts
4. **Workflow Progress Rings** — Circular progress indicators for contract review stages

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- **Immediate Feedback:** Actions trigger instant visual responses (toast notifications, progress updates)
- **Contextual Panels:** Right-side panels slide in for approvals, details, and intelligence insights
- **Keyboard Shortcuts:** Power users can navigate via command bar (/analyze, /redline, /approve)
- **Drag-and-Drop:** Upload contracts via drag-and-drop or file picker

**Animation:**
- **Entrance:** Subtle fade-in (200ms) for new cards and panels
- **Transitions:** Smooth slide animations (300ms) for sidebar and panel toggles
- **Hover States:** Lift effect (2px shadow increase) on interactive cards
- **Loading:** Elegant pulse animation for processing states
- **Progress:** Smooth progress ring animations as agents complete tasks

**Typography System:**
- **Display Font:** Playfair Display (serif) for section headers — conveys legal authority
- **Body Font:** Inter (sans-serif) for all body text — clean, readable, modern
- **Font Hierarchy:**
  - H1: Playfair Display, 32px, 700 weight — Page titles
  - H2: Playfair Display, 24px, 700 weight — Section headers
  - H3: Inter, 18px, 600 weight — Subsection headers
  - Body: Inter, 14px, 400 weight — Main content
  - Small: Inter, 12px, 500 weight — Labels and metadata
  - Code: Fira Code, 12px, 400 weight — Contract clauses and legal text

**Component Styling:**
- **Cards:** 8px border-radius, soft shadow (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08))
- **Buttons:** 6px border-radius, full-width on mobile, icon + text on desktop
- **Inputs:** 6px border-radius, 1px border in muted color, focus ring in tealime
- **Badges:** 4px border-radius, compact padding (4px 8px)
- **Dividers:** 1px solid in border color, subtle visual separation
